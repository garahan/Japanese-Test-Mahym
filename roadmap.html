Minna no Nihongo II (Units 26–50)

This site — Flashcards + Tests
Daily flashcard review (SM-2 spaced repetition) and kanji/vocab/grammar quizzes. Use every day — even 10 minutes counts.
Daily SRS
▶️
Comprehensible Japanese (YouTube)
Search "Comprehensible Japanese N4" or "beginner playlist". Watch 15–20 min daily. Do NOT use English subtitles — use Japanese or none.
Listening immersion
📰
NHK Web Easy (nhk.or.jp/news/easy)
Real Japanese news written for learners. Has furigana. Read one article per day from lesson 35 onwards — builds reading speed naturally.
Reading practice
🎯
Bunpro (bunpro.jp)
Spaced repetition for grammar points. Free tier covers N5+N4. Drill each grammar pattern until you can use it, not just recognize it.
Grammar SRS
📝
Nihongo So-Matome N4 (series)
Start this from Lesson 40+ or when preparing for JLPT N4 exam. 5 books: kanji, vocab, grammar, reading, listening. 6-week daily format.
